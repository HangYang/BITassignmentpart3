During this period of completing the project and cooperating with this team, actually, there is no quicker way for us to achieve the most aspect of our project. We all went down to earth doing every stage in order to finalize the project. When we started planning, it was based on what skills and knowledge that the team members really have, and what skills and interests they wanted to learn and acquire. Therefore, the estimation of how to complete the project and how many features we can achieve is relatively reasonable and “over generous”. However, one thing that we found out a faster way to finish one of the features that we planned might be the MVF1, which is Account Management. For the reason that one of our members has experience of making the Account Management feature. She can just simply adjust some data and then graft onto the new web structure that our project already created, which saved a lot of time for completing the project.
