Extended Viable Feature 1 - Contact Table

Lucid Textual Explanation:
When customers come to our website, once they have any problems, they can contact us through the contact us page. They need to fill in all the information and leave the message they want to tell us. After that, a check form will be displayed so that customers can check whether their information is correct. When we receive the email, we will try our best to solve the customer's problems.

User Story:
As a customer:
I want to talk to individuals with this web organization and ask some questions,
so that I can go to the Contact us page to see they provide for me with information about how to contact them.

Validation Criteria:
Given:
I am on the Contact us page
I can see all the information that I need to fill out
When:
I fill out all the information and messages on the contact table
I check whether there is blank information not filled in
Then:
I click on the submit button
I can see the check form to check whether my information is correct or not.
Extended Viable Feature 2 - Search

Lucid Textual Explanation:
When customers come to the website of our store page,because there are so many game products, they can search the keywords through the search interface to find the game products they want. It's a more convenient and faster way through search. It aims to improve the speed of customers to obtain and collect information.

User Story:
As a customer:
I want to look for product that I want to buy,
so that I go to the search interface to find out product more accurately.

Validation Criteria:
Given:
I am on the search interface
I can see all the game product below
When:
I’m typing the keyword through search button
I’m clicking on search button
Then:
The game product that I want appears on the screen.
Extended Viable Feature 3 - Navigation

Lucid Textual Explanation:
When customers head to our website, they can quickly transfer from one page to another when browsing a website through the navigation bar. The navigation bars appear on each page of the website, customers can go to different pages to find what they are looking for through navigation. It is a faster and easy way for users to use.

User Story:
As a customer:
I want to go to different pages to visit this online gaming store,
so that I can use a navigation bar to easily access the content that I need.

Validation Criteria:
Given:
I am on the navigation bar
I can see all the page name in navigation bar
When:
I click on one of page on the navigation bar
Then:
It transfer from one page to another page very quickly
