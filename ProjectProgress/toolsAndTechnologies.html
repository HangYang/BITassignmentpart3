Team Management, Communication and Documentation:
Our group has a management system and an editor that can be shared:
  • Trello:

    • It is a digital signboard allowing the team members to find the weekly tasks and the details of the tasks that everyone needs to complete, and provide reminders for the due dates of tasks and milestones.

    Link to Trello: https://trello.com/b/GrrGPFy7

  • Microsoft Teams:

    • We had formal and informal meetings in teams. The weekly fixed meeting time has been carried out here. At other times, we will also discuss problems here. By turning on the camera and microphone, we can take care of our emotions and thoughts. And work together to discuss, everyone is participating, making our work more efficient.
